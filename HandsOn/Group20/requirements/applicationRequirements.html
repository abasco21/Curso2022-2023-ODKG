App Description

We will develop a data driven research tool focused on different private collaborative entities registered in Madrid City Council records. Users will be able to make queries based on some of the following criteria: by name, by the kind of entity the selected ones are, by the location of its headquarters, by the geographical area in which they act, trough the existing relationships between them, by the social topic the entity covers or by its number of affiliates, among others. Moreover, users will be able to obtain some useful information associated with queried entities, as adress, phone, email, website, register date or CIF code.

Interface:

We will show locations of all different entities in a map. The number of entities displayed on it will vary as filters are applied. Clicking on an entity locator, relevant information regarding it will be indicated.
